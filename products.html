Sheep & Goats

We are a family-run both sheep and goat farm, dedicated to raising healthy, happy sheeps and goats and providing fresh, high-quality products straight from our pastures to your plate. Whether you’re looking for milk, meat, breeding stock, or just a farm visit—we’ve got something special for you.

Cow Milk

We provide pure, fresh, and farm-direct cow milk with a taste you can trust. Our cows are ethically raised in clean, natural surroundings, ensuring every drop of milk is rich in nutrition and flavor. Whether you're looking for daily milk delivery or bulk supply, we’re here to serve your family or business with the best.

Country Chicks

We specialize in naturally raised, antibiotic-free Country Chicken that’s full of flavor and packed with nutrition. Our birds are raised in open spaces with natural feed—just like in the good old days. No shortcuts, just real taste and real health.
